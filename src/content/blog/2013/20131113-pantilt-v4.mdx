<p>
  A few photos and brief video showing showing new interface and latest iteration of my custom
  pan/tilt video system. This is a small experiment inside of a larger software framework for
  interacting with physical devices. I’ve also been using it to test out some new UI ideas.
</p>
<p>

  construction (and availability of the off-the-shelf components) has changed quite a bit since the
  days of [version
